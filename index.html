Three States, Three Rulebooks

We speak your state's compliance language

Pool safety law isn't uniform across Australia. We hold current knowledge of all three frameworks we operate in.

QUEENSLAND

Form 23 Pool Safety Certificate

Issued by a licensed Pool Safety Inspector under the QDC MP 3.4 pool safety standard. Valid for 1 year on shared pools, 2 years on non-shared pools. Non-compliant pools receive a Form 26 Non-Conformity Notice.

NEW SOUTH WALES

Certificate of Compliance

Issued after inspection against the Swimming Pools Act, and registered on the NSW Swimming Pool Register. Required for sale, lease and new pool sign-off.

VICTORIA

Form 23 — Barrier Compliance

Issued following council-registered inspection under Victoria's pool barrier reforms. Registered pools are re-inspected on a set cycle to stay compliant.

Common Questions

Before you book

How much does an inspection cost?

A standard inspection and Form 23 Certificate is $350. If your pool doesn't pass the first time, re-inspection once the items are fixed is $190 — not a second full inspection fee. Rectification work is quoted separately.

What happens if my pool fails?

You'll get an itemised rectification report with clear timeframes. Once the items are fixed, we re-inspect just those items — you're not paying for a full second inspection.

Do I need a certificate to sell my property?

In all three states, a valid pool compliance certificate is generally required before you can sell or lease a property with a pool or spa. Rules and timeframes differ by state — get in touch and we'll confirm what applies to your situation.

How long is a certificate valid for?

It varies by state and pool type — for example QLD certificates run one or two years depending on whether the pool is shared. We track your expiry date and contact you ahead of renewal either way.

Do you travel to regional areas?

Yes. In QLD we cover everywhere from Cairns down to the Gold Coast, inland through Toowoomba and the Darling Downs. See the Service Areas page for the full list.
