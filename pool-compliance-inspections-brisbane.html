Local Knowledge

Local pools and how we inspect them

Typical properties and pools

Classic Queenslanders on stumps where decks and stairs complicate the barrier, new estates with first-certificate sign-offs, and a large investor market of rental pools on the one and two year QLD certificate cycles. Deck balustrades doubling as pool barriers are the most common Brisbane complication.

The local process

Book online or by phone. We inspect against QDC MP 3.4, give you a verbal summary on site, and lodge the Form 23 with the QBCC register on pass. Non-compliant pools receive a Form 26 with clear timeframes and a $190 re-check.
