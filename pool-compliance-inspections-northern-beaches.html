Local Knowledge

Local pools and how we inspect them

Typical properties and pools

Coastal homes with frameless and semi-frameless glass barriers, sloping blocks where effective barrier height changes across the yard, and salt-air corrosion on hinges and latches that quietly kills gate self-closing. Older beach houses often carry original fencing that predates current standards.

The local process

Book online or by phone. Salt-affected gate hardware is the most common Northern Beaches failure, so test your gate closes and latches from a resting position before we arrive, if it does not, that is likely on the report. Verbal summary on site, certificate lodged on pass.
